TrashTrace

TrashTrace is a comprehensive waste management platform designed to address various aspects of waste disposal and recycling. The platform offers multiple products each aimed at promoting effective waste management, easy segregation, incentivizing users and stakeholders, and reducing littering in public places.
The proposed methodology outlines the development process for a prototype smart dustbin, which automatically segregates biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste using AI and sensors. This intelligent waste segregation system, along with the software applications developed for mobile and web platforms, empowers individuals and businesses to make informed waste disposal decisions. The main goal of TrashTrace is to be the one stop solution for all waste management needs right from the start of the supply chain to the end consumer and recycling agencies

About us

TrashTrace is a complete platform for everything related to Waste Management from source to end of its lifecycle and potential recycling
Some Products under TrashTrace:

Trash Tag

This will allow users to scan QR codes on products and merchant dustbins to redeem incentives for proper waste disposal. The backend server will handle the data related to waste disposal, incentivization, and tracking of products properly disposed of after use.

BinOcculars

This will provide an easy-to-use interface for users to find specific-purpose dustbins for various types of waste, such as electrical waste, plastic, old papers, and metal waste. The backend server will manage the database of dustbin locations and waste types, enabling seamless navigation and waste disposal for users.

LitterPatrol

Utilize AI to detect individuals who litter in public places or UNESCO Heritage Sites. Users will be required to scan their face or ID on entry, and the CCTV cameras equipped with AI will identify and penalize those who litter. The backend server will store the data related to littering instances and fine receipts.

ReCyclX

This software portal will allow users to book appointments for exchanging recyclable materials for cash compensation. The backend server will manage the appointments, recycling centers, and compensation tracking.
